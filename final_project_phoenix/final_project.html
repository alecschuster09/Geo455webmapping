Map Info

Explore the world's top 10 marathons by participation. Toggle layers, search by name, and use the time slider to discover when each race takes place.

Layers
📍 Marathon Locations
Click any marker to see the race photo, participant count, and a link to the official website.
🔴 Marathon Routes
GPS-traced 26.2-mile course for each race, sourced from Strava.
⭕ Proportional Circles
Larger circle = more finishers. Hover to highlight and see participant count.
🎨 Course Difficulty
Color-coded 1–5 rating. Green = easy, red = very hard.
📅 Race Calendar (Time Slider)
Turn on this layer, then drag the slider (bottom-right) to reveal marathons month by month.
