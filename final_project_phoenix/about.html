What & Why

This map shows the top 10 marathons in the world by participation. I ran my first marathon in 2025 and wanted to explore other major races — where they go, how hard they are, and when they happen. The goal is to help runners compare races and pick their next one.

Map Layers

  • 📍 Marathon Locations — click for photo, participants, and official website
  • 🔴 Race Routes — GPS-traced 26.2-mile courses from Strava
  • ⭕ Proportional Circles — bigger circle = more participants
  • 🎨 Course Difficulty — color-coded 1–5 rating (green = easy, red = very hard)
  • 📅 Race Calendar — time slider reveals marathons month by month

Data & Methods

  • Route GPX files downloaded from Strava, converted to GeoJSON in ArcGIS Pro
  • Participant counts sourced from official race websites
  • Difficulty ratings and race months researched and added manually

Limitations & Next Steps

  • Only 10 races — a full global marathon dataset wasn't available
  • Participant counts are from a single year and may change
  • Would like to add elevation profile charts and improve mobile layout
